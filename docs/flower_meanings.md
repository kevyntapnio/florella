bouquet scoring system.

COLORS

- Also optional attribute. Treated similarly to the secondary attributes. Not all requests may require a color

BOUQUET REQUESTS

- A request must require at least 1 main meaning, and up to 3 combinations.
- A request may require secondary attributes and colors.
- A request must specify size: Small (3-5 flowers), Medium (6-8 flowers), Large (9-12 flowers)

Example request:
	 - "My best friend's going through something right now and I want to cheer her up. Keep it small and soft."
		- requirements: Friendship, Comfort, Soft

SCORING AND PRICING

- Base sell price is calculated as the total sell price of all flowers included in the bouquet
- A 30% multiplier is applied when core meaning is fulfilled
- For requests requiring more than 1 meaning: 30% applied if at least 1 is fulfilled
- An increment of 10% is added to the multiplier for every meaning fulfilled. Ex: 40% for 2 meanings, 50% for 3.
- Failure to meet any of the required meanings result to 30% deduction to base sell price.
- For simplicity's sake, Secondary Attributes and Color have their own multipliers. 10% each to base price.
- No negative effects to price for not meeting Secondary or Colors.
- Diversity: 5% multiplier for using varied flowers
